注塑成型專用

Injection molding

PN100是注塑成型專用的PETG產品。注塑的流動性優異，以滿足使用PETG注塑的客戶。目前用在肉厚較厚的產品（例如:霜罐）以及較薄表面積較大產品（例如空調面板）。

In the PETG family, PN100 is processed exclusively by injection molding.   Good flowability during injection meets the need of customers using PETG for thick wall products ( e.g. lotion Jar ) and articles with large area but with thin wall thickness ( e.g. air-conditioner shield )

PN200 is processed by injection molding.  Flowability is superior to PN100 which is suited to make very thick wall products ( e.g. cream jars ) or very thin articles ( such as mobile phone display )
