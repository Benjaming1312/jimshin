ECOZEN是什麼材料？它是透明耐高溫生物基共聚酯ECOZEN是韓國SK化工公司研發的一種新產品，它是PETG/PCTG基礎上升級的耐高溫透明的生物基塑膠。

ECOZEN具有優良的性能，除了具備PETG/PCTG所有的優良性能外，還具有耐高溫100°的特性，從而在食品包裝、嬰幼兒用品等方面具有廣泛的應用前景。 ECOZEN可以用於代替玻璃、PC、PMMA、PVC、透明ABS、透明PP、PS、AS等材料。

What kind of material is ECOZEN ? It is a transparent, high temperature resistant bio-copolyester developed by Korean SK Chemical. A new and premium product developed base on PETG/PCTG which exhibits higher temperature resistance bio-based plastic.

  • 高安全性 Safety
    特別適用於嬰兒食品容器使用，因為沒有具有內分泌干擾物如BPA（雙酚A）。
    It is particular suitable for use in baby bottles because it is non- toxic and BPA-free which is known to disturb human’s endocrine system.
  • 易生產性 Good Processability
    ECOZEN可以在低溫下加工，相對於PC可縮短加工時間，提高了生產效率。
    Ecozen can be processed at lower temperature relative to PC, and cycle time is shortened while productivity is raised.
  • 光學性佳 High Clarity
    ECOZEN具有高透明度，因此適用於PET瓶，水瓶，化妝品容器等。同時因為ECOZEN是透明的，因此可配製成任何需要的顏色。
    Ecozen has high transparency and good colorability and is used in PET containers, water bottles, and cosmetic containers.
  • 功能特性 Functional characteristics
    1.高耐熱性（85~110℃），專門用於高耐熱產品
    2.高耐化學性，ECOZEN產品可廣泛應用於各類洗滌劑及化妝品的香水或霜類等產品
    1.High heat resistance ( 85~110℃ ) allows high temperature applications.
    2.Good chemical resistance allows Ecozen to be used in detergents, body cream, and perfumes jars and bottles.
