MITSUBISHI

《TEFABLOC》TEEE樹脂(TPEE) TPEE resin

產品名稱 Product

熱塑性聚酯彈性體(Thermoplastic Polyether Ester Elastomer)

產品介紹 Introduction

  • 產品特色 Product featuresⅠ
    A系列擁有以往聚酯彈性體所缺乏的柔軟性，加強了與硬質樹脂的熱貼合性可貼合的硬質塑膠 : PC、PC/ABS、ABS、AES、AS、PS、PMMA、 PET、PETG、PA。
    A series of products that offer better flexibility than conventional TPE and increase the thermo-bonding strength with hard resins . Examples of hard resin are :PC, PC/ABS, ABS, AES, AS, PS, PMMA, PET, PETG, PA .
  • 產品特色 Product features Ⅱ
    B系列的優點是耐熱性、耐油性、耐摩耗性、強度佳、強化塗裝性、強化加水分解性。
    單色塗裝用規格特徵
    • 塗裝性-調整添加劑配方，去除會影響塗裝密著性的因素。
    • 成形性-獨家的合金技術，大幅提升成形性，有效控制毛邊。
    • 表面硬度-高度的表面硬度即使相同的彈性率（折頁特性），也可展現高度的表面硬度⇒提升耐油性、耐傷性，二次成形用規格特徴
    • 與PC、PC/ABS、ABS熱接合性良好
    • TEFABLOC高硬度Grade（60A～60D）改善了黏著力的問題

    B series of products offer high heat resistance, oil resistance, wear resistance, good tensile strength, good coat-ability, improved hydrolytic stability.
    Single-color coating specifications
    • Coatability - Adjust the additives formulation to eliminate the factors affecting adhesion.
    • Moldability - Unique technology on alloying, increase significantly the moldability and control the roughness of the weld lines.
    • Surface hardness - Despite the same elasticity ( foldability ), these products exhibit superior surface hardness => increase the oil resistance, scratch resistance, and secondary processing characteristics.
    • Good thermobonding with PC, PC/ABS, and ABS.
    • o TEFABLOC high hardness grades ( 60A ~ 60D ) improve adhesion problems.
  • 產品特色 Product features Ⅲ
    改善一般烴烯類無法達成的高黏著效果與聚酯類、PVC、金屬的黏著性優良，在不影響EVOH物性、成型性下使EVOH柔韌。
    Improve the inferior adhesion strength of polyolefines and have good bonding with polyester, PVC and metal. Without altering EVOH’s properties, molding with TPEE makes EVOH flexible.
