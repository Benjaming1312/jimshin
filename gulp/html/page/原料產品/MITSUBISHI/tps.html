《TEFABLOC》TPS樹脂 TPS resins

產品名稱 Product

苯乙烯系熱塑性彈性體樹脂(Styrene-based Thermoplastic Elastomer )

產品介紹 Introduction

TEFABLOC具有優良的永久回復彈性性能、永久拉伸性能和較好的機械強度。

TEFABLOC exhibits excellent permanent elastic recovery, permanent elastic property, and good mechanical strength.

  • 產品特性 Product features
    • 壓縮永久變形: 70℃×22Hr 30～45%
    • Duro硬度A: 15～95
    • 斷裂點強度: ～30Mpa
    • 斷裂點伸長度: 500～1000%TEFABLOC耐油性優異
    • 具備與氯丁二烯相同的耐油性TEFABLOC具有優良的耐候性及耐熱性。
    • 由於沒有基質聚合物中的雙鍵,因此耐候性及耐熱性優良,具有良好的平衡耐熱性及透明性TEFABLOC擁有符合醫療、食品衛生性的規格
    • Permanent deformation under compression : 70 C x 22 hrs 30~45%
    • Duro hardness A : 15 ~ 95
    • Break point pressure : ~ 30 Mpa
    • Elongation at break : 500 ~ 1000% TEFABLOC has excellent oil resistance.
    • With similar oil resistance as 2 Chloro-butene TEFABLOC has excellent weather resistance and heat resistance.
    • Without double bond in the polymer gives TEFABLOC good weathering resistance and heat resistance. A good balance of transparency and heat resistance TEFABLOC meets the requirements for medical and food sanitary uses.
