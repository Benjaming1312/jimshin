產品特點Features

現在三菱化學正在開發的透明生物工程塑料“DURABIO™”在透明性等特性方面超越了現有的以雙酚A為原料的聚碳酸酯樹脂（PC樹脂），更因為使用了與PC樹脂不同的具有獨特的脂環式氧結構（非芳香族結構）的植物萃取原料， (a)不僅光學特點優異（雙折射低・透明度高・亮度高），還具有(b)耐熱性、(c)耐光性良好、(d)表面硬度高（鉛筆硬度）、(e)剛性強、(f)生物塑膠度等，相折衷的功能、特點並存的特徵。

DURABIO™雖然是植物萃取的聚合物，卻具有以往生物分解聚合物所沒有的優異耐久性。三菱化學計畫充分發揮這些特點，將其廣泛應用於最尖端的光學與能源相關部材，高性能玻璃換代品材料，電子儀器、汽車箱體、內外裝材料等領域中去。

The new bio-based engineering plastic “ DURABIO” currently developing by Mitsubishi Chemical has superior transparency and various characteristics distinct from polycarbonate resin (PC) which is made from bis-phenol A as raw material. Furthermore, it utilises a plant-derived raw material with a uniquely important oxyalicyclic structure ( non-aromatic ) different from PC resin, it has a) excellent optical properties ( low birefringence, high transparency, high gloss ), b) heat resistance, c) good light fastness, d) high surface hardness ( pencil hardness ), e) good stiffness, f) ductile, a balance between bio-materials and plastic characteristics and performance.

Despite DURABIO is a plant-based polymer, it has excellent durability and is not biodegradable. .Mitsuibishi Chemical is planning to fully explore these characteristics to find more uses in optics and energy, substituting high performance glass applications , electronic equipment, and automotive exterior and interior components.
