POKETONE(POK)

POKETONE - 工業材料新指標

  • 高耐衝擊性 High impact strength
    與原有工程塑料材料尼龍、PBT相比，耐衝擊性強2.3倍以上。
    POKETONE has high impact strength taking advantages of relatively flexible CO/Olefin polymer backbone
  • 高耐化學性 Excellent chemical resistance
    POKETONE在塑膠材料中對化學物質的對抗性最強。
    尤其耐水解性優秀，因此幾乎不發生水分引起的物性變化。
    POKETONE is highly resistant to automotive fluids, hydrocarbon solvents, salts, and weak acids and bases.
  • 高耐磨損性 Excellent wear resistance
    POKETONE與現存的最堅固材料聚縮醛(POM) 相比，耐磨損性優秀14倍以上(以樹脂原料為準)，屬於半永久性材料，無需進行部件更換。
    POKETONE has excellent wear resistance even better than POM and reduces various noise issues.
  • 高阻隔性: 氣體阻隔性能 Exceptional barrier properties
    POKETONE的氣體阻隔性能(gas barrier)在現存的高分子材料中最優秀，與被使用為食品包裝材料的EVOH是同等水準
    The gas and hydrocarbon barrier properties of POKETONE is exceptional as similar to EVOH that is at the highest level of all existing polymers.

同時安全性上也已取得了FDA、歐盟(REACH、RoHS)、NSF61、ISO 16949等多項認證。這樣的優異的材料將是您未來在產品開發時的領先材料。

POKETONE has obtained FDA, EU (REACH, RoHS), NSF61, ISO 16949 and other certifications in terms of safety. Such excellent materials will be your leading materials in product development in the future.

POKETONE 具有良好的流動性，並且不需要進行回濕就能具有良好的韌性及強度。
POKETONE has good fluidity, and can have good toughness and strength.

取得美國FDA FCN認證及歐洲食品可接觸認證，優異的安全性及材料物性讓使用者更安心。
POKETONE has obtained the US FDA FCN certification and the European food contact certification.
