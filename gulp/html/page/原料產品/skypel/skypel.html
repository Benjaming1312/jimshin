SK 熱塑性聚酯彈性體(thermoplastic polyester elastomer TPEE)“SKYPEL”

公司提供的韓國SK熱塑性聚酯彈性體（TPEE），為多項零件和組件提供致勝的性能組合，它具有橡膠的彈性、塑膠的強度和熱塑性塑膠的易加工性；性能可與杜邦公司Hytrel產品相媲美，價格實惠，性價比高，對需要具備優異撓曲疲勞性和寬範圍使用溫度的零件，選用該產品是極為理想的。

We offer Korean SK Chemical Thermoplastic polyester elastomer ( TPEE ) for the manufacturing of parts and components, it exhibits rubber’s flexibility, the strength of plastics and the ease of conversion of thermoplastics. SKYPEL has performance similar to Hytrel of DuPont. SKYPEL has good value proposition and for those applications which require high fatigue resistant, resilience, and flexibility at low temperatures and good retention of properties at elevated temperatures, it is an optimal material to use.

    • 優異的抗彎曲疲勞性能
    • 極強的耐撕裂，抗蠕變性

    • 良好的耐磨性能,回彈力好,反撥性高
    • 出色的耐沖擊性能,尤其是在低溫(-40℃)

    • 硬度範圍大（30D-82D），著色方便
    • excellent bending fatigue resistance
    • excellent resistance to tear and to creep.

產品應用 Applications

TPEE主要用於要求減震、耐沖擊、耐曲撓、密封性和彈性、耐油、耐化學品並要求足夠強度的領域。如：聚合物改性、汽車部件、耐高低溫電線護套、液壓軟管、鞋材、傳動皮帶、旋轉。成型輪胎、撓性連軸節、消音齒輪、電梯滑道、化工設備管道閥件中的防腐耐磨耐高低溫材料等

TPEE is mainly used in areas to provide shock resistance, impact resistance, flex-resistance, seal-ability, elasticity, resistance to oils and chemicals and good toughness. Applications are : plastic compounding , automobile parts , hight and low temperature resistant cable sheath, oil-pressured flexible hose , shoe parts , conveyor belt, rotary wheel, ?tire , flexible shaft assembly , silencer gears , escalator belt, and components in pumps used in chemical plants which are chemicals resistant, abrasion resistant and able to withstand wide range of temperatures.
