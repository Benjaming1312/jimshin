PCTG offers better physical and mechanical properties, especially in impact strength, which is similar to or already exceeds PC.   JN100 is replacing PVC in capacitor packaging as one of the more environmental friendly substitutes.   JN200 is widely used in replacing PC ( when high temperature resistant is not required ) to produce injection products which need good mechanical properties.
