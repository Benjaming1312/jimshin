MITSUBISHI

《ZELAS》烯烴類熱塑性彈性體樹脂 Thermoplastic Elastomer

產品名稱 Product

烯烴類熱塑性彈性體樹脂(Thermoplastic polyolefin)

產品介紹 Introduction

在聚丙烯的基體中，使烯烴類橡膠（EPR）細微分散而形成的高性能熱塑性彈性體

ZELAS is produced by inserting olefinic rubber (EPR) along the polypropylene backbone to form a high performance thermoplastic elastomer.

  • 比重小，產品輕量化
  • 耐熱性及優良的柔軟性
  • 與聚烯烴有良好的相溶性
  • 具有良好的平衡耐熱性及透明性
  • 強度高
  • 耐衝擊性優良
  • 容易剝離
  • 符合醫療要求
  • 適用於各種滅菌方式（γ、EB、AC）
  • 柔軟性
  • 透明性
  • 耐熱性: 110～165℃
  • Low density , light weight product
  • Heat resistance and good flexibility
  • Good compatibility with polyolefines
  • Good balance between heat resistance and transparency.
  • Good tensile strength
  • Good impact resistance
  • Easy to peel apart
  • Meet medical requirements
  • Suitable for all sterilisation methods ( Gamma ray, EB, AC )
  • Good flexibility
  • High transparency
  • # Heat resistance : 110 ~ 165℃
