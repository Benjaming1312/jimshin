ECOZEN

ECOZEN

 

ECOZEN是什麼材料？它是透明耐高溫生物基共聚酯ECOZEN是韓國SK化工公司研發的一種新產品，它是PETG/PCTG基礎上升級的耐高溫透明的生物基塑膠。

ECOZEN具有優良的性能， 除了具備PETG/PCTG所有的優良性能外，還具有耐高溫100°的特性，從而在食品包裝、嬰幼兒用品等方面具有廣泛的應用前景。 ECOZEN可以用於代替玻璃、PC、PMMA、PVC、透明ABS、透明PP、PS、AS等材料。

What kind of material is ECOZEN ? It is a transparent, high temperature resistance bio-copolyester. A new product developed by Korean SK Chemical which exhibits higher temperature resistant than PETG/PCTG.

Ecozen has excellent properties and on top of the characteristics of PETG / PCTG, it can withstand up to 100 ℃ temperature. That opens up a wide variety of applications in food packaging and baby use products.

  • 特性 Characteristics
    耐衝擊增強，優良的透明度
    可耐高溫 85度~110度
    透明度可取代PMMA、PVC、PC
    High impact resistance, good clarity
    Temperature resistance 85~110 deg C
    Can replace transparent plastics such as PC, PMMA and PVC.
  • 應用範圍 Applications
    複合應用，薄膜，片材，抽塑應用
    Compounding, sheets, films and profile
  • 應用實績 Application examples
    化妝品容器、家電外殼、嬰兒玩具、文具、食品容器、安全隔板、電器及電子產品部件、保鮮盒、廣告看板、LED看板
    Cosmetic containers, home appliance housing, baby toys, stationaries, food containers, safety partition, electrical and electronic accessories, crisper and food preservation containers, sign board, LED board.

減碳、可回收的環保材質
Lower Carbon emission and recyclable material

ECOZEN原料中使用了來自可再生植物的生物碳源，以減少來自石油碳源的需求。同時ECOZEN可在回收後再次利用，讓您輕鬆為環保盡一份力。

Ecozen uses biomass which is renewable carbon source to reduce the demand of carbon content from fissile fuel. At the same time, Ecozen can be recyclable which helps your environmental protection effort.

優異的清澈度和光澤感
High clarity and gloss

ECOZEN的清澈度和光澤感，可與水晶玻璃媲美，將為您的生活帶來高質感的視覺感受。

The clarity and gloss of Ecozen is comparable to crystal which brings good visual enjoyment to your daily life.

安全、安心（不含BPA）
Safe and worry-free ( non-BPA )

ECOZEN完全不含會影響人體的化學物質雙酚A（BPA），因此您可以放心使用。

Ecozen is completely non-toxic and BPA-free which allows worry-free usage.

優異的抗衝擊性及耐刮性
Good impact resistance and abrasion resistance

ECOZEN優異的抗衝擊性，不論在受壓或者摔落下都能大幅降低因意外而導致受傷或事故的風險，加上優異的抗刮特性，能使產品外觀常保如新，延長產品的使用壽命

The excellent impact strength and shatter resistant of Ecozen reduces the probability of human damages when the finished product is compressed or under a fall. In addition, its good abrasion resistant gives the end product its lasting look which also lengthens the usable life.

熱水⇔冷凍都OK
100°C to -20 °C is acceptable.

ECOZEN的耐熱和耐寒性為100°C至-20°C。適用於各種場合，如熱飲、冷飲。並支持洗碗機清洗，可以重複使用。

Ecozen can withstand boiling water up to 100 °C and also endure -20 °C. It is suitable for use in hot and cold drinks. The wide temperature resistance window allows Ecozen to find use in dish washing machine undergoing repeated high and low temperature cycles.

超輕巧
Lightness

ECOZEN製品是相同玻璃產品重量的一半，讓您不論在使用或清潔上都更加省力。

Products made from Ecozen weighs half of those from heavy glass. It saves energy in cleaning and handling.

優異的耐化學及耐染色性
Excellent chemical and stain resistance

ECOZEN優異的耐化學及耐染色性，讓您無論在盛裝酒精性產品，或是易染色的咖哩或咖啡等，以及使用的清潔產品，這些都可以不用擔心影響產品外觀及特性。

Ecozen’s good stain resistance allow usages in alcoholic drinks, curry and coffee while its chemical resistance allows use in soap and detergents without affecting the outlook and performance.
