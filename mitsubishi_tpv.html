產品名稱 Product

熱塑性硫化烯烴類彈性體(Thermoplastic Vulcanizate)

>

產品介紹 Introduction

  • 產品特點 Features
    • 100%可回收利用
    • 優越的耐化學性
    • 可簡單地進行熱塑性加工
    • 優越的壓縮形變/高溫彈性恢復性能
    • 原始設備製造商規格到客戶定制化配方
    • 可以以預染顏色、自然色或常規黑色來進行供貨
    • 材料高度耐紫外線
    • 既有定制的高流動性/低流動性牌號，也有改進的耐劃傷、耐磨順牌號
    • 具有較大的硬度範圍，從邵氏硬度45A到50D都可以正常供應。
    • 可作為TPO表皮材料的原料進行使用
    • 易加工
    • Trexprene™產品可以用於注塑、擠出和吹塑工藝，普通的熱塑性成型設備即可加工。
    • 只要未經污染，投料入口、材料流道處的殘留材料或是掉落報廢的零件，均可以回收利用。
    • 100% recyclable
    • Excellent chemical resistance
    • Easy thermoplastic processing
    • Superior Compression Set / Elastic Recovery at elevated temperatures
    • Offer standard to tailor-made grades
    • Supply grades in pre-colored, natural or standard black color
    • Highly UV resistant materials
    • Offer standard high flow / low flow grades but formulations with improved scratch & mar, anti-wear resistance are also available
    • Wide range of hardness grades from 45 Shore A to 50 Shore D are commercially available.
    • Can be used as a component of TPO skin formulations
    • TREXPRENE products can be used in injection, extrusion and blow moulding with typical thermoplastic molding equipment.
    • Sprues & runners, off-fall or scrap parts can be recycled back in provided they are kept free from contamination.
