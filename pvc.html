VINNOLIT

德國Vinnolit PVC樹脂型號特性與行業應用
Vinnolit PVC resins characteristics and applications

Vinnolit是德國最大的PVC一體化企業，於1993年成立，PVC糊樹脂和PVC樹脂的總產能約每年80萬噸。日前員工約1400人，年銷售額超過九億歐元。並已通過IS09001管理和IS014001環保認證。

Vinnolit是全球最大的PVC糊樹脂生產廠，年產能超過30萬噸，Vinnolit為了配合不同客戶的生產工藝和最終產品性能要求，研發了一系列的特殊性能產品，現時銷售的糊樹脂型號超過30種。

Vinnolit, established in 1993, is the largest integrated PVC manufacturer in Germany. The total production capacity of suspension and emulsion PVC resins is 800,000 MT per year. Current employees numbers 1400 and annual sales amount to 900 M euro and has obtained ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 environmental certification.

Vinnolit is the largest emulsion PVC resins producer globally, annual capacity exceeds 300,000 MT. In order to satisfy customers’ diverse production conditions and the performance requirement of end products, Vinnolit has developed and is selling over 30 different grades of specialty emulation resins.
