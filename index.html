ABOUT US
錦欣貿易創立於 1988年 , 一直秉持著“專業服務．誠信經營”的服務宗旨，提供來自全世界著名品牌之塑膠原材料，服務遍及包含了化粧品包材、食品包材、紡織、電子與工程塑膠件等產業。

並針對不同產業的所需的不同材料及工藝技術提供完整的技術團隊支持，以期用最適當的原料及工法協助您開發及生產，努力爲您提供最優質的服務與您一同邁向成功。
JimShin Trading Co. Ltd. was established in 1988. Our business philosophy through all these years has been to provide services which are professional and with highest integrity. We supply raw materials from major global plastic suppliers servicing a large variety of industries such as cosmetic packaging, food packaging, textile, electronic and engineering plastics.
We have technical teams to support the use and processing of different raw materials for various industries and markets, so that our customers can find the best solution in design and development as well as production. We collaborate closely with customers with our expertise to achieve ultimate success for both sides.
